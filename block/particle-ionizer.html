Particle Ionizer

The Particle Ionizer is capable of reducing some kinds of matter into tiny ionized particles, charged with a lot of energy. Upon colliding with each others at high speeds they burst, creating a small but powerful magnetic field. Frequencies and particle velocity can be adjusted to let some kinds of entities through it.

In order to work, the Particle Ionizer requires matter (either liquid, solid, or a mix of both) and energy (Redstone Flux). If the necessary matter has been inserted, it will start working, filling it's internal tank with Ionized Particles, which can be pumped out by any standard fluid system.
